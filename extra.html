Extras

Like many artists, Fleetwood Mac has been featured in several popular movies and TV shows. They were featured in Forrest Gump during the run across American scene where "Go Your Own Way" can be heard playing in the background.


They were also featured on the hit show The Sopranos in the episode "D-Girl" where "Rhiannon" plays in the background. Not to mention, their songs have also been used in shows like Family Guy and South Park.


In 2020, a TikTok video by Nathan Apodaca, known online as Doggface, went viral as he cruised down a street on his skateboard while enjoying some cranberry juice and lip-syncing to Fleetwood Mac's "Dreams." The relaxiveness of the clip, combined with the timeless track, struck a chord with millions of viewers, sparking a surge in popularity for both the video and the band.
